- **Art & Set Design** - Set builds, props, dressing
- **Costume & Wardrobe** - Fittings, continuity, laundry
- **Hair & Makeup** - Looks, SFX makeup, timing

### **Camera & Lighting**
- **Camera Department** - Shot logs, camera reports

- **Grip Department** - Rigging, safety, movement

### **Sound & Special**

- **Special Effects** - Method statements, safety
- **Stunts** - Rehearsals, risk controls

### **Post & Marketing**
- **Post Production** - Dailies, edit versions, deliverables
- **Publicity & Marketing** - EPK, stills, social media
- **Asset Vault** - Version control, checksums
